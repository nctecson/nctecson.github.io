Winter Wonderland themed Wedding

December 19, 2017 Nicole Caroline Tecson

It's Snowing! A perfect day for me to share my inspiration blog of a winter wonderland themed wedding.

Disney has always had a very prominent spot in my heart, growing up to tales with happy endings. I loved it. In 2013, Walt Disney Studios produced another amazing film, Frozen. I was inspired by their creativity and decided to collect pictures and present them as my vision for a luxurious frozen-themed wedding.

If I could describe this collection in only three words, it’d be: majestic, captivating and refreshing.

The white wedding gowns are styled just like Queen Elsa's of Arendelle, and with the organza-like cloak hanging from the shoulders it gives off a royal expression. Complimenting the lace sheath silhouette, we have the bridesmaids' baby blue maxi dress with versatile necklines.

Nothing would make a wedding more sweeter than, well... sweets. Beautifully decorated and tasty desserts would be a big hit alongside your grand wedding cake. From left to right; macarons, cupcakes, cake pops and rock candy.

One of the most important aspect of a wedding is the decor. Without a theme, design and style you may find yourself lost in a mis-matched disarray and colours that contradict with each other. Blue, white and silver lead this Winter Wonderland theme as it represents a cool season.

An assorted bouquet with calla lilies, elegant ice bar and an indoor celebratory firework for your first dance completes the finishing touches of your Winter Wonderland with a "wow" from your guest and a happily ever after for you and your true love.

Let it snow, let it snow, let it snow!
